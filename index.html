Hello There!

My name is Shannon Shaw and I'm a UI/UX, Motion Designer + Illustrator.
I believe that curiosity and creativity go hand-in-hand and I approach every situation with an open mind.

Today, I am a recent UX intern and a third year student at Algonquin College studying graphic design. I've been a freelance artist for over ten years and I'm proficient in several mediums including acrylic, watercolour, charcoal, graphite, Adobe Illustrator, InDesign, After Effects and Photoshop. In recent years I've learned to code with html, css, jekyll and javascript, I use this to code my websites using Github and Atom.

In my free time I'm an avid gamer who is all about player-vs-environment gameplay. I'm also a passionate reader of comic books and a solid Druid in D&D. My dream is to find a design family where I can build fluid experiences, illustrate, and create solid projects with great people.
