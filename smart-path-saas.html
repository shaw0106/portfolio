SAAS Website

The Challange: Design and code a SAAS website that is 100% responsive.

The Solution: SMART-PATH is SAAS that teaches users of all skill levels to code, html, css, javascript, yaml, and more. After extensive research I decieded to target a high-income market of users who are looking to advance their careers in programming. I used a monochromatice colour palette to stand out from my competition. I created a brand that used the logos 3 round cornered square as its cornerstone and followed this through by creating buttons and images in the same shape.
