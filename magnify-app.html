Magnify App

The Challange: Create an ios app that students could use to improve their lives.

The Solution: I conducted a survey for students to understand their anxieties and fears in order to create an app that could help them solve these problems. Through this survey I discovered that a vast majority of students were worried about their readiness to interview for jobs.

Using this information I could then ideate possible solutions to the problem statement and research the competition in order to create a vibrant, responsive app that will help students gain the skills and confidence to interview for their future jobs.

My Strategy

To create an app that helps students I created a clean, simple hierarchy of information with strong iconography keeping the interactions simple.

Using a system of positive reinforcement combined with a gamified feature which allows the user to collect badges for progress, confidence will be gained through repetition and practice, and will give the user the upperhand in future interviews.
