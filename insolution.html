UI/UX App Design

The Challange: Create and design an Android app that includes a wearable such as a watch. Then create a presentation board to show off the whole process.

The Solution: Knowing that diabetics want to take control of their health I created Insolution to sync with a smart watch that logs and records data such as, sleep, glucose levels and exercise. The user records their mood, insulin intake and calories and can display the data in order to compare and understand the relationship between sleep and mood.

Presentation Board

For the final hand-in we were tasked with creating a presentaion board in order to collect feedback on the effectiveness of the app and the visual design.
