Insolution

The Challange: Create and design an Android app that includes a wearable such as a watch. Then create a presentation board to show off the whole process.

The Solution: From my research I gathered that diabetics want to be in control of their health. I created Insolution to sync with a smart watch that logs and records data such as, sleep, glucose levels and exercise.

The user records their mood, insulin intake and calories and can view the data on the dashboard in order to compare and understand the relationship between each health factor.

For the final hand-in we were tasked with creating a presentaion board in order to collect feedback on the effectiveness of the app and the visual design.
